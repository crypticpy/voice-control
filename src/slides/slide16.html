PRO TIPS

Getting the Most from SuperWhisper

1

Speak Naturally

Talk at a conversational pace. Whisper handles natural speech patterns, pauses, and filler words gracefully.

2

Use Pauses Strategically

Brief pauses help with punctuation. Longer pauses (2-3 sec) often trigger paragraph breaks automatically.

3

Custom Vocabulary

Add technical terms, project names, and acronyms to improve recognition of domain-specific language.

4

Master the Hotkey

Mac: Hold Fn key to record
Windows: Ctrl+Shift+Space
Customize in preferences for comfort.

QUICK TIP: PUNCTUATION

Say "period", "comma", "question mark" for explicit punctuation. Or let Whisper auto-punctuate based on context.

QUICK TIP: ENVIRONMENT

Quiet environment helps accuracy. A good microphone matters more than model size for clarity.

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS

Escape = Cancel recording
Check Preferences for full shortcut list
