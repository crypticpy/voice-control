IMPORTANT LIMITATION

Wispr Flow is Cloud-Only

Unlike SuperWhisper, Wispr Flow does NOT offer local/on-device processing. All audio is sent to cloud servers.

What This Means:

Audio always leaves your device
Requires internet connection
Not suitable for air-gapped environments

WHAT PRIVACY MODE ACTUALLY DOES

Zero Data Retention

With Privacy Mode ON:

Audio/transcripts NOT stored on servers
Data NOT used for model training
Processed then immediately deleted

With Privacy Mode OFF (default):

Audio/transcripts may be stored
Data may be used for AI training
History accessible in your account

FOR GOVERNMENT/PHI USE:

Wispr Flow's Privacy Mode provides zero retention but audio still goes to cloud. For true local processing where audio never leaves your device, use SuperWhisper with local models instead.
