RECOGNITION SETTINGS

Vocabulary & Replacements

VOCABULARY WORDS

Help AI recognize custom terms DURING transcription

Use for: Acronyms, names, brand names, technical terms, non-English words

REPLACEMENTS

Auto-correct text AFTER transcription (programmatic, not AI)

Use for: Persistent errors, expanding abbreviations, formatting fixes

EXAMPLE REPLACEMENTS

"todo" → "TODO"
"use state" → "`useState`"
"APH" → "Austin Public Health"

VOCABULARY TIP

Keep vocabulary list SHORT. Too many words can confuse the AI transcription model.

HOW TO CONFIGURE

ACCESS VOCABULARY

1. Click menu bar icon
2. Open Settings
3. Go to "Vocabulary" tab
4. Add words or replacements

REPLACEMENT BEHAVIOR

Case-insensitive matching
"todo" matches "ToDo", "TODO", "Todo"
Output appears exactly as you specify
Works 100% reliably (not AI-dependent)

MODEL COMPATIBILITY

Vocabulary hints work with most models.
Exception: Nova and Parakeet models don't support vocabulary hints.

Prefer replacements over vocabulary for consistent results
