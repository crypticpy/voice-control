Brainstorming
Session

THE GOAL

Kick around the idea with your agent. This is collaborative ideation, not specification.

DISCUSS

What's the core intent?
Who is the audience?
What are the objectives?
What would success look like?

Voice is perfect for brainstorming

HOW TO BRAINSTORM WITH AN AGENT

1. STREAM OF CONSCIOUSNESS

Just talk. Describe what you're imagining. Don't worry about structure or completeness.

2. ASK FOR PUSHBACK

"What am I missing?" "What problems do you see?" Let the agent challenge your assumptions.

EXAMPLE VOICE PROMPTS

"I'm thinking about building a tool that..."
"The main problem I want to solve is..."
"My users would be people who..."
"What do you think about this approach?"

FOLLOW-UPS

"Can you poke holes in that idea?"
"What similar products exist?"
"How would you scope an MVP?"

SAVE THE OUTPUT

Ask the agent to summarize the brainstorm into a markdown file. Commit it to the repo.

DON'T SKIP THIS

Rushing to code without brainstorming leads to building the wrong thing. Investment here saves time later.
