PHASES 1-5

Discovery and experimentation. High human involvement, lots of voice interaction with the agent.

PHASES 6-7

Formalization. Lock in standards so the agent can work autonomously later.

PHASES 8-10

Execution mode. Agent does heavy lifting. You review and ship.

Key Insight: The first 7 phases are investment. They enable phases 8-10 where the agent becomes highly autonomous.
