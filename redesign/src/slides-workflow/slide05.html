Phase

THE MISSION

Launch comprehensive research efforts. Build deep context before building anything.

RESEARCH TARGETS

Subject matter expertise
Current best practices
Industry landscape
Competitive products

Key: Save all research artifacts to the repo.

THREE RESEARCH DIMENSIONS

1

Subject Matter Research

Deep dive into the domain. What does the agent need to know to build this well?

2

Best Practices & Patterns

Current industry standards. How are the best teams solving similar problems?

3

Competitive Landscape

What products exist? What works well? What gaps can you fill?

VOICE PROMPT EXAMPLE

"Research the current state of [topic]. Look at what tools exist, how teams are solving this, and what the best practices are. Save your findings to docs/research/"

OUTPUT FORMAT

Markdown files in docs/research/: competitive-analysis.md, best-practices.md, domain-knowledge.md. These become context for later phases.
