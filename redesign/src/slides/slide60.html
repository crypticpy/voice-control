PHI Handling Guide

Protected Health Information requires special handling. Know your boundaries.

SAFE FOR PHI

SuperWhisper with local models (FREE tier works!)
Ollama with local models
Azure OpenAI with BAA in place*
Air-gapped systems

NOT SAFE FOR PHI (without approval)

Wispr Flow (cloud-based, even with Privacy Mode)
Direct OpenAI/Anthropic API calls
Consumer AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude.ai free)
Default OS cloud dictation

PHI INCLUDES

Names, dates, addresses, SSN, medical record numbers, health conditions, test results, provider information, any data that could identify a patient

*Always verify BAA status with your compliance team before use
